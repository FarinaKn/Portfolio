Sombra

2026

Seville — Sun — Heat.

These were the first thoughts that came to mind when thinking about Sombra. Through this photographic series, I want to explore how heat shapes public life in Seville, and how people instinctively seek refuge within shadows while architecture itself becomes shelter, contrast, and structure under the intense southern sun.
