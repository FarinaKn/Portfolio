Cursed Chronicles

2024 – 2025

A university Projektstudium — a 2D animated fairy-tale series in horror style, developed by a team of five. My responsibilities spanned storytelling (concept, screenplay, scene and shot lists for Hansel and Gretel), background design (first draft sketches), social media marketing (content strategy, static and dynamic creatives, performance analysis), and sound design (sound map, sound library, full sound design and foley recordings for both Hansel and Gretel and Sleeping Beauty).

Grade: 1.3

Concept & Strategy

Cursed Chronicles is a 2D animated horror fairy-tale series developed as a university Projektstudium. The social media strategy was built to grow an audience from scratch — establishing the series' dark, editorial tone before the films were released.


Content pillars spanned teaser posts, behind-the-scenes glimpses, character reveals and release announcements. Every post was designed to deepen the world and build anticipation, staying true to the unsettling aesthetic of the series.

Dark & Dramatic
