Social Media

Happy Fit

2023 – heute

Ongoing social media management for Happy Fit — a fitness studio group with four locations in Germany. Responsible for the full creative process, including concept development, content strategy, filming, editing, graphic design and copywriting.

As a certified fitness trainer working at the studio, I also contributed fitness expertise by creating educational content and translating training knowledge into engaging, member-focused posts. All campaigns, visuals and content were developed and produced independently.
