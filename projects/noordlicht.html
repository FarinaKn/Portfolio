Brand Identity

Noordlicht

2025

Complete rebranding for Noordlicht — a nordic-inspired café in Zürich. From a new logo and custom icon set to brand patterns, social media templates, a redesigned menu card and a matching website. The new identity captures the warmth and quietude of Scandinavian design: clean, considered and inviting.

Concept & Strategy

Noordlicht — Northern Light. The brief was to create a new visual identity that felt rooted in nordic tradition while speaking to a modern, urban audience in Zürich. The result is a system built around warmth, craftsmanship and simplicity.


The colour palette draws from natural materials — warm cream, forest green and rich off-black. Typography is balanced between an expressive serif and clean mono elements. Every touchpoint, from the menu card to the Instagram grid, feels part of the same considered world.
