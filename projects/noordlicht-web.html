Web Design

Noordlicht

2025

Website for Noordlicht — a nordic-inspired café in Zürich. Developed as part of a comprehensive rebranding, the site brings the new brand identity to life online: calm, refined and rooted in Scandinavian simplicity. Clean layouts, considered typography and a visual language that feels as warm as the café itself.

Concept & Strategy

Noordlicht is a Nordic-inspired café in Zürich — a place for slow mornings, quality coffee and the quiet pleasure of something homemade. The brief was to develop a complete brand identity and website that capture that feeling: calm, considered and rooted in Scandinavian warmth.


The project spans every touchpoint — from the custom wordmark and hand-drawn food illustrations to a trifold menu, loyalty card, social media templates and a fully responsive website. A coherent visual language designed to travel seamlessly across print, screen and physical space.

Layout Principle
Nordic Minimal

Generous whitespace anchors the layout — sections breathe rather than compete. The custom Noordlicht typeface carries the brand's warmth in headlines, while Inter keeps body text clean and readable. Deep blue is used sparingly as a high-contrast accent, letting the off-white background set the calm, airy tone throughout.
